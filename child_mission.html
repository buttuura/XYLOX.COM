Mission

OUR MISSION:

We listen, asses, and respond to the needs of vulnerable people in our communities to help break the cycle of poverty by providing care, support & protection through shelter, food, and education. OUR Vision Our vision is to ensure that no child suffers or perishes from neglect, abuse or deprivation and that every child grows up in a loving home. PROJECT OBJECTIVES:

  • To provide to orphans and semi orphans childhood that they never experienced and help them to become useful members of the society.
  • To encourage orphans and semi orphans to kindle their potential by providing creative opportunities
  • To make available a secure place where orphans can play and enjoy their childhood.
  • To provide basic education to orphans ,by building both a primary and a secondary school
  • To provide such welfare services like accommodation and health and recreation
  • To provide basic literacy and facilities for those who wish to study further
  • To select and prepare for a vocation in order for the children to live on their own legs
  • To accomplishing physical and intellectual development of the children to strengthen them to become self-reliant in the Home in Kabarole district
  • To provide an opportunity to orphans, and poor children to live in congenial atmosphere with parental love to pursue their education
  • To provide medical care to children living in the orphanage Home CAPACITY: 0ur organization is registered as a Non-Governmental organization. Our organisation has experienced and competent staff to undertake and implement program activities. The Capacity building priorities will upgraded each year on the basis of the need.
